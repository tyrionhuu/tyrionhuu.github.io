My name is Tianyu Hu (胡天羽 in Chinese). I am a senior in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at the University of Science and Technology of China. My research interests center on large language models, and I am also broadly interested in interdisciplinary research areas including computational biology, computational social science, and computational finance.

Besides my academic work, I am also a photographer and videographer. I have been taking photos since 2021 and making videos since 2022. I am also a big fan of classical music.
